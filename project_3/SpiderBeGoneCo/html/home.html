Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a pest control company?

No we don't offer pest control services. We are focused on invasive spiders that you don't want to kill.

Do you have to kill the spider or are there gentler options?

You can filter the method of removal based on your preferences and yes there is a filter to gently release the spider into the wild.

Can I become a Spider Killing specialist?

It's very easy to become a paid spider killer. Just sign up in the contact form and check the box that says "Become a Spider Handler" and whenever you are free set your status to available.
